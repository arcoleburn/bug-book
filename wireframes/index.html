How it Works

Daily Journal

Keep a simple accounting of the events of each day, any notable occurences, and how you spent your time.

Happiness Score

Rate each day on a simple 5 point scale. A -2 is an awful day. A +2 is a fantastic day.

Meaningful Hours

Maybe you're trying to write a book. Or learn a new skill. Or whatever else. Keep track of how many hours you spend in creative/stimulating/intellectual work.

Spot Trends

Over time, Bug Book helps you to spot trends, leading to a better understanding of yourself.
